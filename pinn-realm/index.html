Methodological transparency

How PINN Realm interprets country records

Country association

A country association is derived from the countries reported for author affiliations in the standardized source dataset.

National paper

A paper containing one unique country is classified as national. Country totals include both national and internationally coauthored papers.

International paper

International cooperation is inferred when at least two countries occur in the same paper record. Every unique unordered country pair receives that paper once.

Interpretation limit

Country co-occurrence in affiliations does not necessarily prove direct collaboration between specific institutions or every listed author.

Volume

The number of unique papers shared by a pair of countries under the selected year filter.

Jaccard intensity

Joint papers divided by all papers associated with either country: joint / (A + B − joint).

Country names follow the standardized source file. An explicit mapping connects all 63 source names to ISO 3166-1 alpha-3 codes and the world-map geometry identifiers.
