Atlas / Privacy

Privacy by design

A static atlas,
without tracking.

The PINN Review Atlas does not use accounts, advertising, analytics, tracking pixels, contact forms, or non-essential cookies.

Data stored on your device

The site uses browser local storage for three optional conveniences:

  • Light, dark, or system theme choice
  • Selected-reference reading list
  • Recent bibliography searches

This information stays in your browser and can be removed by clearing this site’s storage.

No authentication or sessions

The Atlas has no user accounts, login system, server session, or profile database. Session timeout and authentication controls are therefore not applicable.

External destinations

DOI, publisher, GitHub, and other external links open third-party websites. Their own privacy and cookie policies apply after you follow those links.

Hosting and transport

The public site is served by GitHub Pages over HTTPS. GitHub may process standard infrastructure logs under its own service policies.

Downloads and clipboard

Citation files are generated locally in the browser. Copy actions use the browser clipboard only after you press the relevant button.

Corrections and contact

Reference corrections are reported through the public repository’s structured GitHub issue form; no personal contact form is embedded in the Atlas.
