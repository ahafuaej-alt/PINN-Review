# World map attribution

The `world-map.json` geometry is derived from the `world-atlas` package, which
converts Natural Earth country boundaries to TopoJSON. Natural Earth data is in
the public domain. The `world-atlas` package is distributed under the ISC
license.

- Natural Earth: https://www.naturalearthdata.com/
- world-atlas: https://github.com/topojson/world-atlas

The map is projected to a 1000 × 530 Natural Earth view for the PINN Review
Atlas. Singapore is represented by a small geographic point marker because it
is not present as a country polygon in the 1:110m source geometry.
